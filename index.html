Pengjie Ren

PostDoc at University of Amsterdam

I work on Neural Machine Translation and Machine Learning for NLP under supervision of Wilker Aziz, Ivan Titov, and Khalil Sima’an. My research is part of the project Machine Translators: Teaching Computers to Translate Using Their Own Words.
